What is Contract Farming?

Contract farming is an agreement between farmers and buyers to produce a specific quantity of a crop at a predetermined price. This arrangement helps farmers secure a market for their produce while providing buyers with a reliable source of supply. It often includes provisions for technical assistance, inputs, and sometimes financing, ensuring that both parties benefit from the arrangement.
